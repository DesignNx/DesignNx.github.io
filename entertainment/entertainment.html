ENTERTAINMENT

Entertainment apps comprise a wide range of interactive activities from purely leisure apps (music, playing games) to communication apps (social media, streaming media, instant messaging).

MovieBook

MovieBook app can help you do way more than just passively accept bookings and payments online. Reservation technology (res-tech) has evolved to the point that it’s become a central hub for managing every aspect of your business, from marketing to distribution to operations.

MusicMix

MusicMix is free digital music service that provides access to over 30 million songs with about 20 thousand songs being added daily and 100 million active users. MusicMix is available on both android and IOS devices however it is locally bound to definite regions.
